Types of Jellyfish

Graceful and mysterious creatures of the ocean

Moon Jellyfish

Recognized by their translucent bell and short tentacles, moon jellies are one of the most common jellyfish species found worldwide.

Lion’s Mane Jellyfish

The lion’s mane jellyfish is the largest known species, with tentacles that can extend over 30 meters in length, making it longer than a blue whale.

Box Jellyfish

Known for their cube-shaped bells and potent venom, box jellyfish are found in the waters of the Pacific and Indian Oceans.
